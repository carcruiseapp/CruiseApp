Support

If you run into bugs, map sync issues, invite problems, or anything confusing during a cruise, this is the place to reach out.

Contact Support

Email: CarCruiseApp@gmail.com

Please include the following when possible:

  • your device model
  • your iOS version
  • what you were doing in the app
  • what happened vs. what you expected
  • screenshots or screen recordings, if available

Common Issues to Mention Clearly

  • joining a cruise
  • map markers not updating
  • route not appearing or appearing slowly
  • background tracking issues
  • leader controls like remove, ban, or end cruise
  • garage/custom car sync issues

Safety Reminder

Use Cruise only when it is safe and legal to interact with your phone. If you are driving, prioritize the road and local traffic laws at all times.
